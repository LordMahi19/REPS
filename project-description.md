	- **Project instructions**
		### AI reported
		AI may be used in completing the task or part of the task. The student must report the use of AI according to the teacher’s instructions. Failure to report the use of AI will be considered academic misconduct.
		The use of AI during ideation is accepted, however you must write and return your own work! Remember to cite any outside resources you may have used while completing your work!
		LUT Software engineering-implemented courses require that all student-submitted work that include design and/or writing tasks need to include a "Declaration of AI usage", or in Finnish "Ilmoitus tekoälyn käytöstä". This Declaration needs to be included in the submitted report, and should list the following:
		1. Name all AI systems that were used in the development of the contents of this project.
		2. How and where they were used (illustrations, proofreading, getting ideas for text, generating diagrams etc.)
		3. Clearly state that no AI assistance or tools were used in this project work.
		This Declaration is a mandatory part of the submission, and leaving it out means that the work is incomplete and should not be graded. Declaring that no AI was used in the development of the document, but getting a high number in TurnitIn AI check is a valid ground for failing the work just by itself.
